What is KaPi-Noy?

Product Service:
Kapi-Noy is an IoT-enabled smart coffee vending machine that accepts recyclable plastic bottles as an alternative payment method. The system combines automated beverage dispensing, plastic bottle collection, and real-time monitoring to promote convenience, sustainability, and responsible waste management within educational institutions and community.

Social Impact:
KaPi-Noy promotes environmental awareness by encouraging proper plastic waste disposal through an incentive-based recycling system. By integrating sustainability with everyday coffee purchases, the project helps reduce plastic pollution, supports recycling initiatives, and fosters a culture of environmental responsibility among students and community members.

How to Use

How do I recycle a bottle? ▾
Simply insert your plastic bottle into the designated slot. The machine will automatically detect, weigh, and validate the bottle. If accepted, you will receive a reward credit.
What types of bottles are accepted? ▾
The machine accepts plastic bottles with sizes ranging from 230mL to 500mL. Accepted sizes include: 230mL, 250mL, 290mL, 320mL, 335mL, 350mL, and 500mL.
How do I get my reward? ▾
After successfully recycling bottles, the machine will dispense a reward token or credit. For every 3 small bottles (230-290mL), 2 medium bottles (320-350mL), or 1 large bottle (500mL), you receive 5 pesos credit.
What if I insert a metal object? ▾
The machine has a built-in inductive sensor that detects metal objects. If metal is detected, the bottle will be automatically rejected and the transaction will be reflected on the screen.

Maintenance

How often should I empty the storage bin? ▾
The storage bin should be emptied when the fill level reaches 80% or higher. The dashboard will display an alert "Almost Full — Need to Empty!" to notify the admin. Regular checking is recommended every 2-3 days depending on usage.
How do I clean the machine? ▾
Use a soft, dry cloth to wipe the exterior surfaces. For the bottle insertion slot, use a slightly damp cloth to remove any dirt or residue. Always ensure the machine is powered off before performing any maintenance.
What should I do if the sensor is not working? ▾
If the sensors are not functioning properly, check the connections and ensure the sensors are clean. If the issue persists, contact the technical support team for assistance.
How to reset the machine? ▾
To reset the machine, simply power cycle the unit by turning it off and on. The system will automatically recalibrate and return to ready state. For any major issues, contact the admin dashboard for troubleshooting.

Dashboard

What does the dashboard show? ▾
The dashboard provides real-time monitoring of the machine. It shows total accepted/rejected bottles, storage fill level, machine operational status, latest transaction details, and bottle size inventory.
How do I view transaction history? ▾
Transaction history is available in the dashboard. You can search by date, weight, status, or bottle size. The table also includes pagination so you can browse through all transactions.
What does "Storage Capacity" mean? ▾
Storage Capacity refers to the remaining space in the bottle collection bin. The machine uses an ultrasonic sensor to measure the fill level, displayed as a percentage on the dashboard.
What is the "Bottle Size Inventory"? ▾
This section shows the count of bottles collected per size category (230mL, 250mL, 290mL, 320mL, 335mL, 350mL, and 500mL). It helps track which bottle sizes are most commonly recycled.
